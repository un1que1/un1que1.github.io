About us

Let me introduce you to a site where the most demanding tourist from anywhere in the world can make a trip to the United States of America.

Today, the United States is one of the most attractive destinations for thousands of tourists from all over the world, including Russia. Fans of various types of recreation open up here for themselves a lot interesting places and attractions. The US tourist potential is so huge that it is unlikely to fit within one trip. In order to see all the natural and man-made miracles created by the human hand, it is necessary to come here again and again!

Any trip without a guide around the country instead of new impressions can bring inconveniences only because of the wrong route chosen or problems with life. Which hotel to choose? Where tasty snack and where to go first? To answer all your questions, and also to save your nerves and time, our specialists of companies and guides will help you.

Do you want to know what the guide is for? Do you want to make your trip or family vacation unforgettable? Then you have found what you need. Professional guides, interesting tours, excursions and expeditions, cultural and historical and culinary tours, as well as fishing, safaris and nature tours - you will find all this on our website.

You can argue that the Internet has a huge number of tourist guides and all information can be learned from the vastness of search sites, but this is not so. All the necessary information, and even the most important and necessary for your tour, you will not receive in full and you will always attend one thought "How to make it all ...". Entrust your vacation to a specialist who will help you plan your holiday, make it as interesting and exciting as possible. After traveling to countries, we very often meet with problems of a similar nature: the climate is different, a completely different pace of life, an unfamiliar speech ...All this unexpectedly falls on us, turning a long-awaited vacation into an endless trial with maps, dictionaries, signs and local dialects.

We tried to make the site easy to use, and, of course, as informative and convenient for travelers as possible. We hope that our site will become your guide and help you in choosing surprisingly beautiful places you want to visit, where you want to stay any tourist, and someone who does not represent your life without travel, new experiences and impressions.

The site consists of several sections, with which you can plan your route, find interesting tours and excursions.
