Gastronomic tours in the USA.

Sounds absurd?

By no means ... New York has long established itself as a restaurant city. The kitchens are presented different, portions are large, the prices are humane, alcohol is poured into cocktails as expected (even more), species restaurants - any capital for envy. History can be "very tasty", at least when it comes to New York and the history of its restaurants. Do not believe that New Yorkers eat only burgers, the inhabitants of the Big Apple are one of the most demanding gourmets of the world, spoiled by the variety and the highest quality of restaurants.

According to the latest estimates, in New York there are about 50,000 restaurants, cafes, restaurants, snack bars and bars. In such a variety it is not easy to orient and choose the best places that will allow not only to eat, but also to get aesthetic pleasure after trying something unusual. We invite you to an exciting journey through the world of the New York cookie.

During the tour we will visit the most "unusual" restaurants of the West Village and Little Italy area, looking at the kitchen to the famous chefs, and tasting their specialties. The best steak, pasta, seafood, cheeses, dissertations, wines - all this awaits you in seemingly unremarkable restaurants designed for local connoisseurs. During the tour we will also visit several restaurants where famous films were shot and we will visit historical pubs.

Try history on a taste - we are confident, you will like it![…]
