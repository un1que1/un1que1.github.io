Individual autotours

    • Feel the spirit of freedom.

    Imagine: From the first until the last day of your stay in America, you travel by car. The Russian-speaking manager will accompany you throughout the whole route and will give all the necessary information about a specific place for self-inspection.

    These tours are offered to those who like to travel independently at the wheel of a car or on motorcycles. The programs offer car rental, overnight stays in hotels along the road and an independent acquaintance with the sights. Rent a car is made by car category, rather than by model or engine size. The driver's age is from 25 years. In the event that the driver is younger than 25, but over the age of 21, please specify the additional amount. All drivers must have their driver's license issued by their country and a return air ticket to their country. International driving license is not accepted. Rented cars can not be used in Mexico. Cars are leased with a filled gas tank. The cost of gasoline is charged to the customer additionally at the rental office at market prices. Return of the car must be made with a minimum amount of gasoline, money for unused gasoline is not returned. Special seats for children under 7 years are provided by the law of the USA and must be ordered during the reservation of the car.
