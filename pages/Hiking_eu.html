Bicycle tours

Central Arizona.

In America they love bicycles, and the developed road infrastructure allows you to ride a bicycle to amazing natural sites! One of the most entertaining destinations in America for a biker is Arizona!

Deserts of Arizona - a "Mecca" for cyclists during the winter and spring! Our tour can start any day of the week from October 15 to May 15. The difficulty level is "medium", and we recommend this cycling for physically trained cyclists. On the route will come up rises, sometimes small, but steep enough. There will also be several long descents. So, leave your cold edge to warm up under the gentle sun and train like professionals, enjoying the western spirit of Arizona!

WHY BIKING IN ARIZON ?!

The climate in Arizona is moderate. The opportunity to see the famous Grand Canyon. The developed road infrastructure and constant change of a landscape - a cycling track among hills and the cactuses growing along all your way! On the bike route you will have a unique opportunity to swim in the Bartlett Lake. The lake feeds the purest waters of the Verde River (Verde Rive), and its area is about 3000 hectares. Visit the local reservation! Fountain Hills. The fountain is located in the middle of the lake and ejects water to a height of 562 feet (171 m). And this is really a magnificent sight! Only in Arizona can you see a meteorite crater with a diameter of 1250 m and visit a museum dedicated to American astronautics and meteorites. And in the end of March you will have an unforgettable sight - Ostrich festival. At the festival you can see various animal shows, concerts, a lot of attractions and, of course, the highlight of the program - ostrich racing![…]
