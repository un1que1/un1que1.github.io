In order to see the dynamic New York, never falling asleep Las Vegas, the boundless canyons of Arizona, the beaches of California and Florida, Russian citizens need to issue a visa to the United States. It is believed that getting an American visa is very difficult: travelers are frightened by long lines, a terrible interview in English and a high percentage of refusals. In fact, if you comply with all the rules and regulations, the issuance of a visa in the United States is becoming a logical and predictable process.

The Consular Section of the US Embassy in Moscow and the US Consulates in Russia issue visas for applicants.

The overwhelming majority of Russians applying for visas receive multiple entry visas for three years - the maximum possible time for applicants at the moment. Entry restrictions, announced by the US Department of State, refer only to a specific group of officials and a number of unofficial persons. These restrictions do not apply to most applicants.

Although in past years the time for waiting for an interview could be long enough, today the waiting time is usually about a week or less.
